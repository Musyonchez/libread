Chapter 7 Xiao Hei Shocked Everyone

"The tacit understanding between the beastmaster and the spirit beast is very important. Someone come up now."

Zhao Tingyun's palm lit up, and a muscular purple thunder leopard covered in dense lightning appeared beside him.

As soon as the purple thunder leopard appeared, it immediately became alert and extended its sharp claws.

Seeing this purple thunder leopard, everyone kept swallowing their saliva, their legs and feet began to become weak, and no one dared to say a word.

Zhao Tingyun frowned, "I won't kill your spirit beasts, so why are you so scared? Li Yuanhu, come up and demonstrate!"

Hearing this, Li Yuanhu's face turned dark and he walked forward tremblingly.

His father is the commander of Tiannan Guard, one level higher than Zhao Tingyun.

But he had heard of Zhao Tingyun's notoriety since he was a child, and he was scared to death.

Rumor has it that none of the evil demons that died at his hands were intact; all were shattered to pieces.

"Let your spirit beast out. You're really embarrassing your father by looking like this." Zhao Tingyun said with disgust.

Li Yuanhu gritted his teeth and released a tiger that was burning with fire.

[Spiritual Beast: Fiery Tiger]

[Potential: Command]

[Level: Black Iron Level 6]

[Combat Power: 201]

[Skill: Flame Roar (Level A, emits a tiger roar, with fire damage and 30% slowdown effect)]

.......

[Spiritual Beast: Purple Thunder Leopard]

[Potential: Command]

[Level: Gold Level 4]

[Combat Power: 25300]

[Skill: Thunder Claw (S-level, claw attack with thunder damage, can be swung four times per second, damage is three times that of a normal attack)

Thunderstorm (Level A, lightning bursts all over the body, and enemies within a five-meter range will be damaged by the arc)

Raikage (B-level, transformed into thunder, speed increased by 500%, duration 8 seconds)]

.......

Two hundred versus twenty-five thousand...

Lin Hao's mouth twitched.

No wonder Li Yuanhu is so afraid of Zhao Tingyun.

"Now tell your little tiger to attack me. You can use any means. As long as it can touch me, you have passed the test." Zhao Tingyun said with a smile.

"Uncle Zhao, your spirit beast is too strong. How could my spirit beast possibly touch you with it here? You are simply bullying the weak!" Li Yuanhu's face turned pale.

Zhao Tingyun shook his head slightly, "My spirit beast won't attack. I just let it out to get some fresh air."

Hearing this, Li Yuanhu's eyes lit up.

The Fiery Tiger is very fast, and its Fiery Roar can slow it down, so it is still possible for it to just hit Zhao Tingyun.

"Fire Tiger, come on!"

The Fiery Tiger roared, and circles of fiery sound waves flew towards Zhao Tingyun. The next moment, its body turned into a shadow.

Zhao Tingyun smiled faintly, and with a move of his feet he easily avoided the range of the sound waves.

The distance between him and Li Yuanhu, which was originally more than ten meters, disappeared in an instant.

Everyone only saw the Fiery Tiger rushing forward, but in an instant Zhao Tingyun grabbed Li Yuanhu with one hand.

Li Yuanhu's face turned purple and he was thrown to the ground the next moment.

Zhao Tingyun shook his head at him in disappointment.

"Faced with an enemy much stronger than you, you actually ordered the spirit beast to stay away from you and put yourself in danger. If you were outside the city, you wouldn't survive for more than three days!"

"But how did I bump into you if I didn't attack? What did I do wrong?" Li Yuanhu asked puzzledly.

"This is your business. You can choose to wait for an opportunity, or even run away. Winning or losing is not important here. Staying alive is the most important thing!

You only think about meeting me, but don't think about your own situation. Do you have a chance to meet me? Even if you meet me, can you escape unscathed?"

Li Yuanhu lowered his head slightly and remained silent.

"Failed, today's training will be doubled!"

Li Yuanhu raised his head instantly, with disbelief in his eyes.

"Uncle Zhao, you didn't say there was a punishment!"

Zhao Tingyun glanced at him indifferently.

"Here, I am the rule. What I say is what counts. Today everyone has to fight me. If you satisfy me, you can make a request. If you don't meet my request, you will have to double your training."

Everyone suddenly let out a burst of ghostly howlings.

Fighting with a gold-level beastmaster is no different from seeking death.

This is too difficult for these newcomers.

Under pressure from Zhao Tingyun's strength, everyone was forced to move forward one by one.

Even Liu Qingyan went up.

Relying on her own talent and the Ice Crystal Bird's freezing and slowing ability, she was just a step away from hitting Zhao Tingyun, but ultimately she couldn't escape the punishment of having her training doubled.

Liu Qingyan walked back with a bitter face.

"Lin Hao, Commander Zhao is so mean, he just wants to intimidate us!"

Lin Hao smiled and said, "What Commander Zhao said is indeed useful. It's not shameful to lose to him. There's no need to be sad."

Hearing this, Liu Qingyan reacted instantly.

He is a gold-level beastmaster, and I am just an iron-level beastmaster. It is normal for me to lose!

"Lin Hao, come here quickly and stop talking like a sissy!"

Zhao Tingyun looked at Lin Hao with a somewhat cold tone.

Lin Hao walked forward and released Xiao Hei.

As soon as Xiao Hei appeared, everyone exclaimed.

"This... is this still the Fire Demon Dog? How come it's so tall and so domineering!"

"Bullshit! This is not a fire demon dog at all. The fire demon dog is not this big, and its eyes are not blood red!"

Liu Qingyan gently covered her mouth, somewhat unable to believe it.

Lin Hao's fire dog was obviously not like this seven days ago. How come it changed so much after just a few days of not seeing it?

Zhao Tingyun let out a sound of surprise and was also a little confused about Xiao Hei's appearance.

"Lin Hao, is your spirit beast a fire demon dog?"

Lin Hao nodded slightly, "It is indeed a fire demon dog, but it has a big appetite. After eating too much meat, it became like this. It seems to have mutated a bit."

There are many mutated spiritual beasts on Blue Star.

These spiritual beasts usually break away from their original species and become a completely new species.

Moreover, mutated spirit beasts are generally much stronger than ordinary spirit beasts, and are also more rare!

"Not bad, not bad, this spirit beast is very outstanding, and its future achievements will not be low. You must cultivate it well!"

Zhao Tingyun has a very strong mental strength and can detect the difference in Xiao Hei just by sensing it.

Xiao Hei's domineering and cold aura was so strong that it was hard to ignore.

"Let me see the strength of this mutant spirit beast. If it satisfies me, it can eat as much as it wants!"

Big appetite? Here, only spirit beasts will die of overeating!

Hearing about food, Xiao Hei suddenly became excited, and the dark hellfire on his body burned even more fiercely.

As soon as the fight started, Lin Hao immediately gave orders.

"Xiao Hei, Burning Heaven Devil Flame!"

The next moment, a large amount of pitch-black magic flame spewed out of Xiao Hei's mouth.

The devil flame burned fiercely, enveloping Lin Hao and Xiao Hei.

People in the distance couldn't help but shiver.

"Such a cold flame! Why is there still a cold flame? I feel like I am deep underground!"

"Me too. This chill penetrates deep into my bones. Even my thinking has slowed down!"
