Chapter 13 Killing the Third-level Demon and Getting a Surprise Reward

After walking nearly a thousand meters, we saw a huge crocodile demon with a body length of more than five meters lying on the ground to rest.

Lin Hao opened his eyes of insight and looked.

[Name: Fierce Crocodile (Demon)]

[Level: Third-order demon]

[Combat Power: 5300]

[Skills: Bite (B rank), Crazy Crocodile Tail Swing (B rank)]

[Note: This ferocious crocodile from a foreign land was transformed by the evil spirit. It has strong defense, is invulnerable to swords and guns, and has an amazing bite force. If you don't want to be eaten, stay away! ]

Lin Hao took a breath of cold air.

The combat power of 5300 is more than five times that of Xiao Hei!

Liu Qingxue took a look at the ferocious crocodile and had some thoughts in mind.

"This ferocious crocodile is five meters long, probably just reaching the level of a third-level evil demon.

A top-level third-level ferocious crocodile can reach eight meters in length.

It cannot be killed unless it is a gold-level spirit beast.

Let's kill the other evil demons around first, and then attack it together!"

Then Liu Qingxue looked at Lin Hao, "You can command the spirit beasts to attack those first-level evil demons, and leave the rest to us."

Lin Hao nodded, "No problem."

After the tasks were assigned, everyone rushed out instantly.

Each of their spirit beasts attacked a second-level evil demon, trying to kill it as quickly as possible.

Xiao Hei ran out quickly, waved his claws, and instantly killed several first-level demons.

Xiao Hei, who has reached the eighth level of Black Iron, has become several times stronger and can easily kill a first-level evil demon.

The ferocious crocodile immediately opened its eyes, crawled on the ground on its short limbs, and headed towards the black and brown lion closest to it.

"Nana, tell the black and brown lion to stay away. The ferocious crocodile's attack power is very terrifying!" Liu Qingxue reminded loudly.

Nana nodded slightly and directed the black and brown lion to stay away from the fierce and ferocious crocodile.

After a few minutes, all the low-level demons around were killed, leaving only the ferocious crocodile surrounded in the middle.

Liu Qingxue took a deep breath, condensed a stream of cold air in her palm, and then blew it towards the ferocious crocodile.

The Ice Jade Python also exhaled an icy breath from its mouth.

A patch of ice and snow condensed on the ground. The ferocious crocodile's limbs slipped slightly in the ice and snow, and its crawling speed slowed down a lot.

"Everyone attack together and break its defense first!" Liu Qingxue commanded.

The next moment, nine spirit beasts including the Ice Jade Python, Iron Skin Rhino, and Black and Brown Lion all began to attack.

There was a sharp sound, and sparks flew everywhere.

The ferocious crocodile had bloodshot eyes and swept its huge tail, sweeping the iron-skinned rhinoceros away, knocking down several large trees before it stopped.

Lin Hao immediately used the Fountain of Life talent to heal it.

The Iron Rhino immediately got up vigorously and continued to rush forward.

"Thank you." Zhou Yu thanked in a dry tone.

Lin Hao didn't care and continued to direct Xiao Hei.

He also wanted to see if he could gain demon points and demon-eliminating progress by working together to kill the demons.

If possible, the benefits gained from killing the third-level evil demon will be absolutely huge!

In the distance, the sharp claws of the spirit beast collided with the skin of the ferocious crocodile, making a sound of metal colliding, but it did not break.

"Qingxue, the defense of this ferocious crocodile is too high. We seem unable to break it." Chu Tianhao frowned.

There were so many of them, taking turns to attack, and even their attacks landed on the same spot, but they still failed to break the defense.

Alligator leather is so strong!

Lin Hao's eyes lit up, and he immediately ordered Xiao Hei to use the Hell Claws!

The Hell Claw ignores defense. Although it cannot cause much damage to the ferocious crocodile at present, it can still break the defense.

After all, even Zhao Tingyun, who had a combat power of more than 6,000, had been injured by Xiao Hei!

Suddenly, black flames ignited from Xiao Hei's body, and the cold breath made the other spirit beasts feel a little uncomfortable.

The sharp claws stretched out more than ten centimeters, and then one claw slapped the ferocious crocodile.

Suddenly, an obvious wound appeared, and trickles of black blood gushed out.

The ferocious crocodile couldn't help but roar angrily and flap its tail continuously.

"Oh shit, that's awesome! It can even break the defense of the ferocious crocodile. This spirit beast is too strong!" Chu Tianhao's face was full of shock.

Nana also showed envy in her eyes.

Her black and brown lion only had extraordinary potential, and was still two major levels away from the king level.

The highest level you can reach in the future is gold. If you want to reach a higher level, you must contract a spirit beast with higher potential.

However, Tianyang City will only provide the first spirit beast for free. The subsequent spirit beasts need to be purchased by oneself or caught in the wild.

This is very difficult, and wild spirit beasts are more violent, making contracts more difficult.

If you can find a spirit beast that has just been born or has not yet fully hatched, the difficulty of the contract will be greatly reduced!

"Retreat first, and then concentrate your attack after the ferocious crocodile calms down!" Liu Qingxue continued to command.

Everyone continued to attack according to this tactic.

Xiao Hei continuously used the Hell Claws to enlarge the wound. In the end, the wound was as big as a washbasin and the internal organs could be clearly seen.

Zhou Yu commanded the Iron Rhino to use the horn on its head to hit the wound.

In an instant, the entire rhino's head was knocked in. The ferocious crocodile rolled several times and then died.

[Ding! Cooperate to kill the third-level evil demon, the fierce and fierce crocodile, demon removal points +120, demon removal progress increased by 60%. ]

When Lin Hao heard the system prompt, his face lit up with joy.

Cooperating to kill can also earn you demon-killing points and demon-killing progress!

However, what you get should not be all of it, but only a part of it.

Otherwise, it would be impossible for a third-level evil demon to provide so few demon-killing points and demon-killing progress.

【Name: Lin Hao】

[Level: Black Iron Level 8]

[Talent: Fountain of Life (SSS level), Eye of Insight (SSS level)]

[Combat Power: 130]

[Beast Tamer: Hellhound (Overlord)]

[Demon Elimination Point: 188]

[Demon Extermination Progress: 84%]

The benefits of killing high-level demons are too great.

The demon-killing progress has reached 84%, maybe it will reach 100% tomorrow, then you will get a chance to win a lottery!

And after Lin Hao reaches the silver level, he can contract a second spirit beast.

The speed of killing the evil demons will be absolutely unimaginable!

"Hahaha, we can actually kill a fierce crocodile known for its defense. This is what a third-level evil demon can only do."

Nana stepped on the corpse of the ferocious crocodile with great pride, as if she had killed it alone.

"This time we are all thanks to Lin Hao's mutated spirit beast, otherwise with our attack power we would most likely not be able to break its defense." Chu Tianhao said with a smile.

Liu Qingxue put the ferocious crocodile into the storage ring.

"Lin Hao also participated in the killing this time, and he will also have a share after the sale. Do you have any objections?"

Everyone waved their hands immediately.

"Of course not. If it weren't for Lin Hao, we would probably just be running away now."

"Yes, Xiao Hei is only at the Black Iron level but he can break through the defense of the Silver level demon. This attack power is too terrifying!"

"Many mutant spirit beasts have some incredible abilities. Perhaps Xiao Hei's ability is unparalleled penetrating power. I also want to contract a mutant spirit beast in the future!"

Lin Hao smiled and said, "It's everyone's credit that we were able to kill the fierce crocodile. Xiao Hei could only break through the defense. The big head is still yours."

Liu Qingxue rarely smiled.

"Don't be so modest. The credit belongs to you. Our team distributes the rewards according to the efforts. I will give you your share after selling the evil demon's corpse."
