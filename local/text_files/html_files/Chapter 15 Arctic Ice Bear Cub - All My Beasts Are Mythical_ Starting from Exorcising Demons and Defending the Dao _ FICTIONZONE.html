Chapter 16 King-level Mutated Arctic Ice Bear

After picking up the valuable corpses of the demons, the group walked into the dim cave with excitement.

Liu Qingxue took out a lighting device and turned it on, and the space inside the cave suddenly became bright.

Deep in the cave, a tall, snow-white Arctic bear fell to the ground, no longer breathing.

The five bald little balls whimpered and rubbed the polar bear's corpse, with sadness in their voices.

They may not know that their mother is dead, and all their actions are driven by hunger instinct.

Lin Hao turned on his Eye of Insight, and one of the polar bears immediately caught his attention.

[Spiritual Beast: Arctic Ice Bear (Mutation)]

[Potential: King]

[Level: Black Iron Level 1]

[Combat Power: 12]

[Skills: Violent Bear Rage (S-level), Ice Crystal Body (S-level)]

[Note: During the birth process, it undergoes unknown mutations due to the environment and its own influence. Its body can freely crystallize into ice. Even if it is shattered, it can be reassembled. Its defense is five times that of an ordinary Arctic ice bear! ]

Looking at this little guy, Lin Hao felt a little tempted, but he quickly retracted the thought.

The direction of his second spirit beast has been determined. The quality of this Arctic ice bear is indeed good, but he still plans to give up in the end.

However, this Arctic ice bear is quite suitable for Liu Qingxue.

She also has an ice-type talent, which makes her very suitable for the Arctic ice bear.

As the daughter of the city lord, she has enough resources to train this mutated king-level Arctic ice bear.

Lin Hao looked at the other four polar bears.

Three have the potential to be commanders, and one has the potential to be extraordinary.

This result is perfect!

"What should we do with the body of this polar bear? Do we really want to take it back and sell it?" Xiaodie said reluctantly.

After Tianyuan appeared in the world, the blue planet was filled with blood and darkness.

It is so difficult to see such a scene full of maternal radiance.

And they also want to contract its children.

If you want to develop a tacit understanding with a spirit beast, you must treat it wholeheartedly.

"Let's bury the body of the polar bear in this cave. We've already gotten a good deal by getting five cubs. There's no need to make a little more money." Liu Qingxue suggested.

The others also agreed.

Zhou Yu summoned the Iron Rhino, dug a big hole in the cave, and then buried the Arctic Ice Bear in the cave.

When preparing to carry the five Arctic bear cubs out, Lin Hao gently bumped into Liu Qingxue, then signaled her with his eyes to choose the mutant cub.

Liu Qingxue showed a puzzled look on her face, and it took her a while to vaguely guess Lin Hao's intention.

Before asking her to take care of Lin Hao, Liu Zhen mentioned that he had a second talent.

Insight! The gift of seeing things through the surface!

Lin Hao's abnormality definitely shows that the Arctic ice bear in her hand is extraordinary!

The team walked out of the cave and began to discuss the distribution of the polar bear cubs.

"Qingxue, I want one. You know how much effort I put into the second spirit beast!" Nana spoke first.

Liu Qingxue thought for a moment.

"Let's give priority to those with higher ranks in supplying these Arctic ice bear cubs. Those who get the cubs will need to pay some extra federal coins to compensate others. Do you have any objections?"

Everyone had no objection to this. They could get money and buy spiritual beasts after breaking through the silver level, so they didn't lose much.

After the distribution was completed, Nana finally got what she wanted and hugged a polar bear cub with the potential to be a commander, and she kept touching it with her face.

"Little Bear, I will be your mother from now on, okay? I promise to raise you to be plump and white!"

A slight smile appeared on Liu Qingxue's face.

"Let's stop here for this operation. We don't have anything for the spirit beast cubs to eat, so it would be better to go back early and feed them."

"Also, I will be preparing to break through to the Silver Level Beast Tamer in the next month, so I will not be able to continue to act with you. You can make your own arrangements, but be careful about safety."

Liu Qingxue gave a few simple instructions and then led the team members back to Tianyang City.

.......

After returning to Tianyang City, sell the harvested demon corpses first.

The staff member's mouth opened wide when he saw the five-meter-long ferocious crocodile on the ground.

“Miss, how did you kill this third-level evil demon? This is an evil demon that even veteran silver-level beastmasters have a headache with!”

Liu Qingxue raised her eyebrows and said a few random words.

After leaving the Chamber of Commerce, Lin Hao received his share, a total of 80,000 federal coins.

Four days, eighty thousand!

This is simply huge profit!

Lin Hao quickly used most of it to buy meat and first-level growth potions.

Xiao Hei is not far from the bronze level.

When he becomes a bronze-level beastmaster, Liu Zhen will probably not stop him from going out alone.

Then he can start looking for a suitable second spirit beast!

Before parting, Nana gently tugged at Lin Hao's sleeve.

"Brother Xiaohao, my home is at No. 83 Wutong Road. If you miss me, come find me. I'll leave the door open for you."

Liu Qingxue stretched out her long legs and kicked her.

"He won't miss you, so just give up on this idea!"

After that, she took Lin Hao and walked towards the City Lord's Mansion.

Nana stamped her feet and looked at Liu Qingxue and Lin Hao's departing backs with hatred.

"You don't allow me to soak it, you want to keep it for yourself! How despicable!"

......

Liu Qingxue dragged Lin Hao into her room.

This was the first time that Lin Hao entered Liu Qingxue's room, and in such a strange way.

"Can you explain it now?"

Liu Qingxue stared at Lin Hao with burning eyes, as if she wanted to stare a big hole in him.

Lin Hao coughed twice.

"I just think this cub has great potential and is more suitable for Sister Qingxue."

Liu Qingxue frowned slightly, "High potential? How high can it be?"

Although there is a high probability that the cubs of the Arctic ice bears will be commander-level, the number of Arctic ice bears with the potential to be king-level is less than one percent.

Liu Qingxue hardly ever thought about this.

"It's okay, just at the king level." Lin Hao said lightly.

Liu Qingxue's eyes suddenly widened, and she subconsciously clasped Lin Hao's shoulders with both hands.

"King level! You didn't lie to me?"

Lin Hao made a slight tearing sound and felt a sharp pain in his shoulder.

The pair of jade hands immediately withdrew in embarrassment.

"I think Uncle Liu has already told you about my insight talent, so it can't be wrong."

Liu Qingxue looked at the little ball she had placed on the ground, excitement flashing in her eyes.

The upper limit of a beastmaster comes largely from his own spirit beast.

With this Arctic ice bear, she has a 60% chance of achieving diamond level in the future!

The door was suddenly pushed open and Liu Zhen walked in with an excited look on his face.

Then he picked up the Arctic bear on the ground and kept observing it, with the excitement in his eyes almost overflowing.

"Yes, yes, this is definitely a spiritual beast with king-level talent, but his body seems to be colder than that of an ordinary Arctic ice bear, which is a bit strange."

Lin Hao smiled slightly.

"There's nothing strange about it. It just mutated."

"Oh, mutation."

"What, it mutated!"
