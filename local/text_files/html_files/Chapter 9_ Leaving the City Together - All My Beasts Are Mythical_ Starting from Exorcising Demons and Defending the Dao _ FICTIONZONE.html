Chapter 9: Leaving the City Together

The morning passed quickly.

Xiao Hei's belly was round from eating so much, and he was taking a nap at Lin Hao's feet.

The cafeteria door was pushed open and a huge crowd of people walked in.

Everyone had a look of despair on their face.

Zhao Tingyun is known for his strictness, and life will not be easy following him.

Liu Qingyan's forehead was covered with sweat. She slumped down next to Lin Hao, picked up a glass of water and started drinking.

That was what Lin Hao drank, and he didn't have time to stop it...

"Lin Hao, I'm really tired. I don't want to train anymore." Liu Qingyan said aggrievedly.

She was born with a silver spoon in her mouth, and she has never experienced such hardship in her life.

"It's only a month, just hang in there and it will be fine." Lin Hao comforted.

"So will you train with us tomorrow?"

Lin Hao shook his head. "Probably not. I have other plans."

Liu Qingyan immediately looked at him with resentment on her face.

You ask me to hold on while you go out and enjoy yourself?

See if what you say is human language?

"Lin Hao, come here."

Hearing Zhao Tingyun calling him, Lin Hao walked over immediately.

"I have reported your case to the city lord. He asked you to go back and meet him this afternoon. He will tell you about the next arrangements in person."

Lin Hao smiled faintly, "Thank you, Commander Zhao."

Zhao Tingyun patted Lin Hao with his big hand.

"This is your own strength. It has nothing to do with me. Kill more evil demons in the future and don't ruin this little dog."

Xiao Hei, who originally had a leisurely look on his face, became fierce the next moment.

It let out several dull roars to express its dissatisfaction.

"It is very intelligent. It is very likely to mutate into a spiritual beast with king-level potential. It is expected to reach diamond level in the future!"

Zhao Tingyun's face was full of amazement.

King-level potential! In the entire Tianyang City, only the city lord Liu Zhen has one.

Every time he appears, mountains collapse and the earth cracks, thousands of miles are stained with blood, and he defeats large numbers of evil demons!

......

After bidding farewell to Zhao Tingyun, Lin Hao came to the City Lord's Mansion and walked into Liu Zhen's study.

Liu Zhen looked at Lin Hao with a smile.

"I've heard about your story from Lao Zhao. It's incredible. The fire demon dog mutated after only following you for a week, and its potential is very likely to reach the king level!

ADVERTISEMENT

How did you mutate it?"

If we can find a way to increase the mutation rate of the Fire Demon Dog, the overall strength of Tianyang City will be greatly improved!

"Uncle Liu, I just fed it some pork, and didn't do anything else."

Liu Zhen frowned, a thoughtful look on his face.

"Pork? Is it possible that the Fire Demon Dog will mutate after eating pork? This topic needs to be studied. Perhaps this is a brand new evolutionary plan for the Fire Demon Dog!"

Lin Hao's mouth twitched.

What a bullshit evolution plan. If you had money, how could you buy the worst meat for the spirit beast to eat?

"Uncle Liu, I want to go out of the city to kill the evil spirits!" Lin Hao said in a deep voice.

Liu Zhen sighed, “I guessed it, but I really don’t recommend you to leave the city so early.

The world outside is more dangerous than you think. Although your spirit beast has some strength, it has no chance of survival in the face of the flood of evil spirits!"

"I still want to go outside the city!"

Liu Zhen looked at Lin Hao with inquiry in his eyes.

He also didn't understand why Lin Hao was so determined to leave the city.

Normal people are scared to death about leaving the city.

It’s so peaceful in the city, and there’s a high chance of death if you leave the city. Even a fool would know what to choose!

"I don't know why you're so insistent, but I won't allow you to leave the city alone."

Lin Hao immediately heard the hidden meaning in Liu Zhen's words.

If one person is not allowed to leave the city, does that mean more people can leave the city?

"Uncle Liu wants me to find someone to go out of the city with?" Lin Hao asked.

"If you really want to leave the city, let your sister Qingxue take you. She is more experienced and can protect you if you encounter danger."

Lin Hao felt a headache when he thought of Liu Qingxue's cold face.

But he had no choice to leave the city.

"Thank you, Uncle Liu. I'll trouble Sister Qingxue now."

"Haha, we are a family, so don't talk behind each other's backs. You have to listen to Qingxue's arrangements when you go out. She has a bad temper and I can't control her."

After a brief conversation, Lin Hao left the City Lord's Mansion and went to buy some things needed for the wild, as well as food for Xiao Hei.

After this purchase, Lin Hao's balance has dropped to three digits.

"Xiao Hei, you have to do your best this time, otherwise you won't have anything to eat in the future!"

Xiao Hei scratched the ground, looking ready to attack.

In fact, after Lin Hao made a contract with Xiao Hei, he wanted to go out of the city to kill the evil spirit.

A spiritual beast with overlord-level potential has a certain combat capability even at the Black Iron level.

However, Tianyang City was heavily guarded, and anyone who wanted to leave the city needed approval. Lin Hao had no way of leaving the city without permission.

I chose to show off my skills in front of Zhao Tingyun today in order to demonstrate my strength and get Liu Zhen's approval.

.......

Wait until dusk.

Liu Qingxue found Lin Hao who was teasing Xiao Hei.

"Lin Hao, if you don't follow the training well, why do you have to leave the city?" Liu Qingxue asked in confusion.

"Sister Qingxue, I think my strength is enough to kill a first-level evil demon. Training is useless to me. What I need is fighting."

Liu Qingxue frowned her delicate brows and looked at Lin Hao with a not very good expression.

“After you leave the city, you must follow my instructions. If you violate my orders, you must return to Tianyang City!

Meet at the East Gate at 8 o'clock tomorrow morning. If you are late, we will not wait for you!"

After saying that, she left.

Lin Hao had long been accustomed to this tone and did not feel any dissatisfaction in his heart.

"Alas, my strength is still too weak. When I have the strength of bronze level, I should be able to move freely. Then I must kill the evil demon as soon as possible and rush to the second spirit beast of the silver level contract!"

The more spiritual beasts you have, the faster you can kill the evil spirits.

Xiao Hei, who has the potential to be a overlord, is already so terrifying. If he can reach the totem level, or even the mythical level, then he will be a true forbidden weapon!

At dinner time, Liu Qingyan walked home limply, ate a quick meal, and then went back to her room to lie down.

Training is tiring enough, not to mention having to train twice as hard today.

When I thought that this kind of life would last for another 29 days, I lost all hope in life.

......

Early the next morning, Lin Hao ate something and walked towards the East City Gate.

If he was really late, that woman Liu Qingxue would definitely not pay any attention to him.

When Lin Hao arrived, Liu Qingxue and six other people had already arrived.

"Sister Qingxue, I shouldn't be late, right?"

Looking at the other person's eyes, Lin Hao once suspected that the time displayed on his communicator was wrong.

He had already left home half an hour earlier, so why was Liu Qingxue still looking at him like that?

"We're not late, we're just waiting for the last teammate." Liu Qingxue replied.

Liu Qingxue's team, including herself, consists of eight people, five men and three women, and one did not come.

The five men were all in their twenties. They had calm faces and were chatting softly. They ignored Lin Hao.

The other girl looked comparatively... Lolita, Lin Hao could only think of this word to describe her.

Because she is really pink and tender, both in appearance and in her dress.

With a round baby face and timid eyes, he just glanced at Lin Hao cautiously a few times.

"I'm here. Can we gather later next time?"
