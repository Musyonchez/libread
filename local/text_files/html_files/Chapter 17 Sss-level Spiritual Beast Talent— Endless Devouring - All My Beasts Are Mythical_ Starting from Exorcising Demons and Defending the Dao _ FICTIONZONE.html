Chapter 17 Sss-level Spiritual Beast Talent— Endless Devouring

"What, it mutated!"

Liu Zhen's eyes widened as big as copper bells, and he unconsciously used some strength in his hands.

The little ball began to whimper.

Liu Qingxue quickly snatched the cub from his hands and held it in her arms with a cautious look on her face.

Liu Zhen smiled awkwardly, then looked at Lin Hao.

"Xiaohao, are you sure this Arctic ice bear has really mutated?"

It's no wonder that Liu Zhen was shocked. It was because mutated spirit beasts were too rare. It was possible that only one mutated spirit beast could be found among a million.

But now, Xiao Hei seems to be a mutation. If this Arctic ice bear is a mutant, then isn’t the probability of mutation too high?

Lin Hao nodded affirmatively.

"This Arctic ice bear has indeed mutated, and it is an ice crystal mutation. It can transform into an ice crystal body. It can not only resist damage, but also reassemble even if it is shattered. It has a good talent."

Not bad?

A mutated king-level spirit beast, is it just that?

Your requirements are a bit too high!

Liu Zhen's eyes became softer and softer as he looked at the little Arctic bear.

"It is Qingxue's blessing to get this Arctic ice bear. I will immediately allocate 5 million federal coins to you for its training. Xiaohao will also be allocated 1 million. Xiaohei is also worth training."

Liu Qingxue looked at Liu Zhen coldly.

"Dad, why were you at my door just now?"

When Liu Zhen came back to his senses, a hint of unnaturalness flashed across his face.

"I just happened to pass by and heard what you said. There's nothing strange about it."

After saying this, he left quickly.

The time goes back to ten minutes ago.

Liu Zhen just walked out of the study, and in a blink of an eye he saw his eldest daughter dragging Lin Hao into her room and closing the door.

He was afraid that his eldest daughter would do something wrong.

After all, he knew that his little daughter had always liked Lin Hao.

If things go wrong, a war between the sisters may break out in the future.

Naturally, Liu Zhen would not allow such a thing to happen, so he concealed his breath and hid behind the door to eavesdrop.

And then what happened next.

I originally thought it was a bad thing, but it turned out not only was it not, but it was actually a happy event!

......

Liu Qingxue put the cub beside her and let it play on its own.

She looked at Lin Hao with a probing look in her eyes.

"Lin Hao, since you have the gift of insight, why don't you take this cub for yourself? You know, it's no worse than your Xiao Hei."

Lin Hao disagrees.

"This spirit beast is barely okay, but I already have plans for the second spirit beast, and it's not quite suitable for me."

Liu Qingxue glared at him and then kicked him out of the room.

Anyone who hears this will think it's bragging.

If Liu Qingxue hadn't held back, she would have wanted to kick Lin Hao.

Who said his words were so irritating?

A mutant spirit beast worth over ten million, barely okay...

......

Lin Hao returned to the room, summoned Xiao Hei out, and fed it some growth potions and meat.

Then he lay down on the big bed.

"It's hard to rest in the wild. I'd better wait until I break through the bronze level before going out alone."

As soon as he closed his eyes, he immediately remembered that his demon-killing progress outside the Arctic Ice Bear Cave had been completed, and he still had one chance to draw a lottery that had not been used.

"System, start the lottery."

[Ding! The lottery is in progress... Congratulations on obtaining the SSS-level spirit beast talent—Endless Devouring! ]

[Endless Devouring: SSS talent, exclusive to spirit beasts. Spirit beasts with Endless Devouring can devour everything, and everything can be used as nutrients for devouring breakthroughs! ]

Lin Hao sat up instantly.

As we all know, only beastmasters have talents, and spirit beasts do not have them.

The system can actually grant talents to spiritual beasts!

"System, can spirit beasts also have talents?"

[Host, a spirit beast with mythical talent has its own unique talent, which is drawn by the system. The host can freely give it to a spirit beast, and it cannot be changed after the decision is made.]

"If Xiao Hei now possesses the Endless Devouring talent and evolves to the mythical level in the future, will he still have other talents?"

[Yes, the talent given by the system does not affect the talent of the spirit beast itself. ]

Lin Hao could no longer suppress the smile.

"System, merge Endless Devouring and Xiao Hei!"

[Ding! The SSS spirit beast talent has begun to be swallowed and merged with the hellhound...]

A devouring force suddenly surged out of Xiao Hei, who was eating meat.

An endless black hole appeared inside its body, and the surrounding energy flowed into it on its own.

The last bottleneck of the ninth level of Black Iron was broken instantly, and Xiao Hei successfully broke through to the ninth level of Black Iron!

[Spiritual Beast: Hellhound]

[Potential: Overlord]

[Talent: Endless Devouring (SSS Level)]

[Level: Black Iron 9th Level]

[Combat Power: 1070]

[Skills: Burning Heaven Devil Flame (S-level), Hell Claw (SS-level)]

Xiao Hei, who successfully integrated the endless devouring talent, felt a surge of momentum all over his body and a black hole appeared in his mouth.

He ate dozens of kilograms of meat on the ground in just one bite, and digested it in just a moment.

Lin Hao looked at Xiao Hei with interest, then shifted his gaze to the stool next to him.

It can swallow anything, so...

"Xiao Hei, hurry up and swallow that stool for me."

Hearing this, Xiao Hei looked at his master Lin Hao with resentment.

With a look of resignation on his face, he swallowed the stool in big mouthfuls.

Lin Hao clapped his hands, his face full of smiles.

"That's right. It looks like you can eat whatever you want in the future. This saves me from having to buy meat for you. It's not worth it to spend all my money on you. I don't even have time to enjoy myself."

Xiao Hei whimpered a few times in grievance, then lay on the ground in silence.

Such a good talent is actually used to save money on food.

What an unscrupulous master!

"Don't be so autistic. I need to get some sleep. If you're hungry, go outside and chew on some tree bark. It doesn't matter what you eat now."

After saying that, Lin Hao covered himself with the quilt and slept comfortably.

Xiao Hei walked out of the room alone, secretly gnawed on a few ornamental tree branches to fill his stomach, and his back looked particularly desolate and lonely.

......

Lin Hao didn't wake up until dinner time.

When he sat down at the dining table, Liu Qingxue had already sat down. Only then did Liu Qingyan come back with an exhausted look on her face.

After seeing Lin Hao, those lifeless eyes regained some spirit.

"Lin Hao, you're finally back. I want to go and train with you guys next time!"

"No! You can't leave the city until you reach bronze level!" Liu Zhen frowned and refused.

"Then why can Lin Hao do it? He hasn't even reached the bronze level!" Liu Qingyan said unconvinced.

Lin Hao smiled slightly.

"I am already at the ninth level of Black Iron, and Xiao Hei's strength is now stronger than some bronze-level spirit beasts."

"I......"

Liu Qingyan's face flushed.

We were at the same starting line four days ago.

After these four days, her Ice Crystal Bird reached the fifth level of Black Iron Grade, and it would take another four or five days to break through to the sixth level.

How come you have reached the ninth level of Black Iron?

Who among us is the SS-level talented beastmaster?
