Chapter 1 Everyone Is a Beast Tamer. I Start With God-level Talent!
Blue star.

China.

Twinings No. 1 Middle School.

"Students, I emphasize again that when you awaken your beast taming talent, you must concentrate on visualizing the patterns in the Beast Taming Atlas."

"The more specific and detailed the pattern you visualize, the higher the level of your awakened talent will likely be!"

"As for the importance of the talent for controlling beasts, I believe each of you is very clear about it, so I won't say much. I wish you good luck."

After Principal Dong Yaoqing finished speaking, he stepped aside.

In front of the awakening formation, the students of Class 6 of Grade 3 were both excited and nervous.

The exciting thing is that after awakening their talent for taming beasts, they will be eligible to contract pet beasts and become beast tamers.

What worries me is that I don’t know what level of talent I can awaken.

In this era where everyone tames beasts, the level of talent in taming beasts will, to a certain extent, directly determine their fate.

"Brother Mo, have you chosen your visualization object?" Sun Lei looked at the handsome young man next to him with some concern.

Lin Mo hesitated for a moment, then nodded: "I think I've made my choice."

"What do you mean by considered chosen?" Sun Lei's eyes widened: "Brother Mo, you heard what the principal said. This is not a joke."

"I understand, don't worry!" Lin Mo patted Sun Lei on the shoulder, but only he knew how crazy what he was about to do was.

He is actually a time traveler.

Before traveling through time, I had just received the admission letter from Peking University.

I didn't expect that when I woke up, I would find myself in this world where everyone tames beasts.

Here, anyone who is eighteen years old or above and has passed the beast taming theory test has the opportunity to participate in the awakening ceremony.

As long as you awaken your talent for taming beasts, you will have the opportunity to become a beastmaster.

And if one could awaken a high-level beast taming talent, he would immediately become a sensation and a hot commodity that all forces would fight over, and his future would be bright.

As for the object of visualization mentioned by Sun Lei...

In this world, everyone gets a copy of the "Beast Taming Atlas" issued by the school when they are in the first year of high school.

Inside are portraits of the 108 powerful and exotic beasts known to this world.

When everyone awakens their talents, they must choose one to visualize in order to increase their chances of awakening a high-level talent!

The reason why Lin Mo was so conflicted was because he felt that the exotic beasts in the "Beast Taming Atlas" were not powerful enough.

It’s not that the alien beasts are not strong enough.

Every strange beast that can be listed in the "Beast Taming Atlas" is a terrifying existence that can easily massacre a city.

But in Lin Mo's opinion, those powerful exotic beasts are not even a little bit inferior to the mythical beasts in the earth's legends.

Therefore, Lin Mo came up with a bold idea - to visualize the divine beasts of the earth when awakening.

He had even thought of the object of his visualization—the dragon!

Descendants of Yan and Huang, descendants of the dragon.

For him, there is no more suitable object of visualization than this.

But obviously, there are huge risks in doing so.

Because there are no such creatures as dragons in this world, and there are not even any legends about them.

No one knows what the consequences will be if one visualizes a creature that is not in the Beast Taming Atlas.

If awakening fails as a result, it would be more trouble than gain.

Therefore, Lin Mo's object of visualization has never been determined.

But the awakening ceremony was about to begin, and he had no time to hesitate, so he could only give it a try.

"Zhou Jun, F-level talent."

Looking at the red light on the magic circle, the dean in charge of the awakening ceremony announced calmly.

"Why……"

The student named Zhou Jun walked down the altar with a look of disappointment on his face.

The beast taming talents are divided into nine levels from low to high: F, E, D, C, B, A, S, SS, and SSS.

F is the lowest level and most common talent.

Awakening his F-level talent only meant that he was qualified to become a beastmaster.

"Next, Lin Mo!"

Hearing the name, Lin Mo immediately stepped forward and stood in the center of the magic circle.

"You can start to visualize. Remember to stay focused and not be distracted at all." The dean reminded again.

Lin Mo nodded, took a deep breath, and slowly closed his eyes.

In his mind, the image of a giant dragon slowly began to emerge...

In the myths and legends of the earth, there are many kinds of dragons, including Yinglong, Qiulong, Chilong, Qinglong and so on.

What Lin Mo was visualizing at this time was the ancestor of dragons recorded in the "Classic of Mountains and Seas" - Zhulong!

Zhulong is also called Zhu Jiuyin.

It has a human face and a snake body.

It is red all over and a thousand miles long.

When it opens its eyes, it is day, and when it closes its eyes, it is night.

When it inhales, it is winter, and when it exhales, it is summer.

It does not drink, eat, or rest, and it can call the wind and rain...

According to legend, Zhulong was born according to Pangu's will and was the first giant god to appear between heaven and earth after Pangu's death.

When he first woke up, he saw that the world was desolate and lifeless, and he knew that the long river of time was still frozen in nothingness.

So he used his supreme power to separate the chaos of time, and let the long river of time begin to flow. From then on, rocks had layers, trees had growth rings, and all things had birth and death...

There is also a legend that when Gonggong angrily crashed into Mount Buzhou, although Nuwa cut off the feet of the divine turtle to support the four poles and used her own body to repair the sky, she still failed to prevent the catastrophe of heaven and earth.

After all, the feet of the divine turtle could not replace the pillar of heaven.

As the sky falls and the earth collapses, all living things suffer and wail, the world is about to return to chaos.

At this time, Zhulong appeared.

He used his incomparably huge body to wrap around the entire Buzhou Mountain Range, then went straight up around the pillars, wrapping around layer by layer the giant pillars that pierced the sky and earth.

With his supreme divine power, he forcibly tied up and straightened the giant pillars that had already broken and were about to collapse.

Only then was he able to completely quell the catastrophe between heaven and earth, allowing all living things in the world to escape the calamity.

Then, he resurrected Nuwa with a breath of divine breath, and opened up the 'Heaven Beyond Heaven' above the Nine Heavens for the gods to live in...

From all these things, we can see how powerful Zhulong is!

As these contents emerged in Lin Mo's mind, the appearance of the dragon gradually became clear in his mind.

It was a huge red dragon that was so big that it could not be described in words.

But compared to its incomparably gigantic body, the aura emanating from its body is even more terrifying. The majestic and domineering power, the thousand-fold divine power, and the supreme pressure fill the entire heaven and earth, as if they can make the heaven and earth bow down to it.

At the same time.

A faint red light appeared on Lin Mo, who was at the center of the magic circle.

As soon as the light appeared, it began to become denser at a speed visible to the naked eye.

This instantly cheered up the dean who was in charge of the awakening ceremony.

When you awaken talents of different levels, different colors will light up.

From F to SSS, the corresponding colors are: red, orange, yellow, green, cyan, blue, purple, silver, and gold.

The red light on Lin Mo's body is becoming more intense, which means there is a possibility of turning into other colors.

In other words, Lin Mo is likely to awaken a talent above level F.

"E-level, or D-level?"

The dean's face was full of anticipation.

He certainly hoped that Lin Mo could awaken a higher level of talent.

But there is not much hope.

At Twinings High School, D grade is considered a pretty good talent.

There are more than 3,000 people in Grade C, and only one or two at most every year.

As for the B level and above, he hasn't seen it for many years.

In the history of Twinings High School, there was once a B-level talent.

The next day after the results came out, the student was picked up by someone sent from the provincial capital.

Today, many years later, that man has held an important position in the provincial capital and has become a big shot.

However, just when the dean was full of expectations.

The red light on Lin Mo's body disappeared quickly.

At the same time, the awakening ceremony ended.

"What a pity... With such a rich red light, why couldn't it go any further?"

The dean felt sorry for Lin Mo.

But we can only announce it truthfully.

"Lin Mo, F-level talent!"

"Director, I'm not..." Lin Mo stopped talking halfway.

He really wanted to tell the dean that what he awakened was not an F-level talent.

It was just because of a small accident when he awakened that caused the light on his body to be red.

But when the words came to his lips, he didn't know how to start.

Because he had no idea how to explain what had just happened to him.

And even if he said it, no one would believe it because it was too unbelievable.
